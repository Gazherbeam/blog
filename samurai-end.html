A Brief History

The samurai were the military nobility and officer caste of medieval and early-modern Japan. Their era ended during the Meiji Restoration, as Japan modernized and abolished the feudal system.

  • Samurai code: Bushido
  • Key event: Meiji Restoration (1868)
  • Abolition of samurai privileges
  • Modernization of Japan's military
